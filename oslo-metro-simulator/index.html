Oslo Metro Simulator

Oslo Metro Simulator is a realistic train simulation experience that puts you in control of the Oslo T-bane (Metro). Step into the drivers cab and operate authentic metro trains across detailed recreations of Oslo's underground. Follow speed limits, and ensure smooth, safe journeys for your passengers. The simulator emphasizes immersion and accuracy, featuring true to life stations, rolling stock, sounds, and driving physics inspired by the actual Oslo Metro system. Dynamic weather, day/night cycles and a ticket inspection mode. Whether you're a train enthusiast or a casual player curious about public transport operations, Oslo Metro Simulator offers a calm and rewarding look into the daily life of a metro driver in Norway's capital, Oslo.
